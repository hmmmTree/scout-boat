# Scout-Boat
A boat designed to help map out pollutants in laguna de bay

An autonomous surface vessel engineered to analyze water conditions, manage surface waste, and provide actionable data to mitigate flooding in Taguig City and the Laguna Lake region.

## The Problem: Waterway Management and Flooding
Urban flooding is heavily influenced by the environmental state of local waterways and lakes. Surface waste accumulation contributes to drainage blockages and ecological degradation, directly exacerbating flood risks during heavy rains. To address this specifically in laguna de bay, there is a critical need to understand the exact location for the waste located on the lake.

## The Solution
The Lake Sentinel is an uncrewed, autonomous boat designed to tackle these environmental issues at the source. It serves a purpose:
* **Environmental Monitoring:** Navigating local waterways to map out conditions and collect data on water health.

## Technical Architecture

### Hardware
* **Core Brain:** ESP 32
* **Drive System:** Twin two way 50 amp esc
* **Sensors:** Planning to add PH decter, Turbinity, TDS sensor, etc
* **Chassis:** Custom waterproof housing, 3D printed and coated in surface coat epoxy
* **Operational Sensors:** GPS module

### Software Stack
* **Microcontroller Logic:** C++
* **Computer:** Python
